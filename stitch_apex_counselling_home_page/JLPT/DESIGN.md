# Design System Document

This document outlines the core design principles and token values for our digital products.

## Theme Overview
The theme provides a `light` color mode, focusing on `fidelity` in its color variant.

## Colors
Our brand identity is built around a distinct color palette:
- **Primary Color**: `#001F3F` - This deep blue is the cornerstone of our brand, used for primary actions and key elements.
- **Secondary Color**: `#FF8C00` - A vibrant orange that complements the primary blue, suitable for secondary actions and highlights.
- **Tertiary Color**: `#004080` - A medium blue offering additional depth and accentuation.
- **Neutral Color**: `#F8F9FA` - A very light grey, serving as the foundation for backgrounds and surfaces.

## Typography
A clear and consistent typographic hierarchy is crucial for readability and brand recognition.
- **Headlines**: `manrope` - Used for all major headings to convey a modern and strong presence.
- **Body Text**: `inter` - Chosen for its excellent readability across various sizes and contexts, ideal for paragraphs and detailed information.
- **Labels**: `inter` - Applied to form labels and small interactive elements for consistency with body text.

## Shape
The overall shape of our UI elements contributes to the user experience.
- **Roundedness**: `Moderate` - UI elements feature a moderate level of roundedness (value: 2), offering a balanced and approachable aesthetic without being overly soft.

## Spacing
Consistent spacing ensures a clean, organized, and user-friendly interface.
- **Density**: `Normal` - We maintain a normal level of whitespace (value: 2), providing a comfortable balance between information density and visual breathing room.
